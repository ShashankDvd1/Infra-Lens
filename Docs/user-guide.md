# LandScope AI - End User Guide

Welcome to **LandScope AI**, your intelligent real estate and infrastructure tracking platform.

## 🗺️ Map Intelligence
The **Infrastructure Map** is the core of the platform. It plots all upcoming, under-construction, and recently completed government projects across the city.
- **Filtering**: Use the top bar to filter by `Project Type` (e.g., Metro, Expressway) or `Status`.
- **City Switching**: Use the dropdown in the sidebar to view data for Lucknow, Pune, or Hyderabad.

## 🤖 Ask AI
Our LLM-powered assistant can instantly answer questions about the local real estate landscape.
- "What is the budget for the new Ring Road?"
- "Which areas are appreciating fastest due to the metro?"

## 📈 Area Intelligence
This module aggregates multiple infrastructure projects to give an **Opportunity Score** to specific neighborhoods. A higher score means more combined infrastructure investment is flowing into that zone.

## 🚨 Automated Alerts
When you save a specific area or property type to your **Saved Opportunities**, you are automatically subscribed to our alert system. Whenever a new tender is passed or a project breaks ground matching your criteria, you will receive an email and a live push notification.

## 💰 AI Investment Assistant
Navigate to the **AI Investment Assistant** on the sidebar. Answer a few questions about your budget and risk tolerance, and our LangGraph agents will generate a personalized investment strategy pinpointing exact locations with high expected ROI.

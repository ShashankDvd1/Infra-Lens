# LandScope AI — Problem Statement

## Background

In India, major infrastructure and urban development projects — such as metro expansions, ring roads, expressways, industrial corridors, smart cities, IT parks, logistics hubs, airport expansions, LDA schemes, and Awas Vikas projects — significantly impact surrounding land and property prices.

However, information about these developments is often **fragmented** across government websites, authority notices, news articles, tenders, master plans, and public documents. By the time the average citizen becomes aware of these opportunities, institutional investors, brokers, politically connected individuals, and early investors may already have purchased nearby properties.

As a result, **middle-class buyers, first-time home buyers, and small investors** frequently miss high-growth opportunities.

---

## Core Problem

There is no trusted platform that:

1. **Aggregates** upcoming government and infrastructure projects.
2. **Verifies** projects using official sources.
3. **Maps** projects geographically.
4. **Explains** potential impact on nearby land and property values.
5. **Helps users discover** growth opportunities before prices significantly increase.
6. **Provides** transparent legal and source-backed information.

---

## Vision

> Build an **AI-powered Property Intelligence Platform** that helps ordinary citizens make informed real-estate decisions using verified public information.

## MVP Scope (1 Week)

### City Focus

- **Lucknow only**

### Core Features

#### 1. Infrastructure Opportunity Map

Display upcoming and ongoing projects including:

- Metro projects
- Ring roads
- Expressways
- IT City developments
- Wellness City developments
- New townships
- Logistics parks
- Government housing projects
- LDA developments
- Awas Vikas developments

#### 2. Source Verification

Every project must include:

- Official authority source
- Government notification
- News references
- Project status
- Last updated date

#### 3. AI Project Summary

For each project, provide:

- What is being built?
- Why does it matter?
- Expected impact
- Nearby areas likely to benefit

#### 4. Area Intelligence

Users can search an area and view:

- Nearby projects
- Growth indicators
- Connectivity improvements
- Development activity

#### 5. Ask AI

Example queries:

- *"Where should I buy land under ₹20 lakh?"*
- *"Which areas around Lucknow may benefit from future development?"*
- *"Show legal residential opportunities near upcoming infrastructure."*
